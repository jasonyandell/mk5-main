Test Complete Trick Button in Play Area

This test verifies that the complete trick button appears directly in the playing area.

Test Case: All 4 Players Have Played

When all 4 players have played their dominoes, a prominent "Complete trick" button should appear in the center of the playing area.

Test Complete Trick Button (4 players played)

Expected Behavior:

  1. You should see 4 dominoes played on the green table
  2. A green "Complete trick" button with a checkmark should appear in the center
  3. You should NOT need to switch to the Actions tab
  4. Clicking the button should complete the trick

Test Case: Incomplete Trick

When not all players have played, no proceed button should appear.

Test No Button (only 1 player played)

Expected:

  • Only 1 domino on the table
  • NO proceed button should appear
  • It's player 1's turn (not the human player)
